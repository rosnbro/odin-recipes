Oreo Truffles

Description

Oreo truffles are a simple and delicious treat. The rich pairing of chocolate and cream cheese make for a dense burst of sweet flavor in a bite sized package.

Ingredients

  • 1 package Oreos
  • 1 bar cream cheese
  • 1 bag semi sweet chocolate chips
  • (optional) white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Crush Oreos in a food processor
  2. With the processor running, slowly add all of the cream cheese, running until homogeneous
  3. Remove mixture from food processor and form into 1.5" - 2" balls
  4. Refrigerate truffle balls until set
  5. Melt chocolate chips
  6. Dip oreo balls into melted chocolate, covering completely
  7. Refrigerate until the chocolate shells have hardened
  8. (Optional) Melt white chocolate chips and drizzle on top for garnish
